## Positioning

**For:** WoW guild officers and tryhard raiders on private servers  
**Who:** Track and compare DPS/HPS, analyze raid performance, identify improvement areas  
**Unlike:** uwu-logs (site is slow/ugly), warcraftlogs (WoW retail only)  
**Pizza Logs is:** Fast, clean, guild-owned analytics — your data, your server

## Guild Acquisition Strategy

**Now:** Build for PizzaWarriors. Make it excellent.  
**Next:** Word of mouth — other guild officers will ask "what tool is that?"  
**Then:** Simple onboarding flow — any guild can upload their log in 2 min  
**Later:** Guild directory, public leaderboards, cross-guild boss rankings
